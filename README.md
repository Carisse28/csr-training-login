# CSR Training Hub

> **Knowledge and understanding lead to confidence, and confidence naturally leads to speed.**

Welcome to the **CSR Training Hub** project.

The CSR Training Hub is a self-paced training and knowledge resource designed to help Pizza Hut Customer Service Representatives learn, practice, and confidently use the tools and processes required to provide excellent customer service.

More than just a training website, the Hub is intended to become a central resource that representatives can return to whenever they need guidance, updates, reference material, or continuing education throughout their time working with the call center.

---

## Project Goals

The Hub is designed to:

* Build confidence through understanding.
* Provide step-by-step learning experiences.
* Make training engaging and approachable.
* Serve as an ongoing knowledge resource.
* Support continuous learning as systems and processes evolve.

---

## Planned Features

Current and planned sections include:

* POSupreme Training
* Momentum/CoreNexa Training
* Scheduling Resources
* Promotions and Specials
* Store Reference Guides
* Ordering System Issue Reporting
* Troubleshooting Guides
* Knowledge Base
* Interactive Quizzes
* AI Practice Customer Calls
